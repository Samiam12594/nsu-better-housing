Warning! Be careful to read over this page to ensure the information displayed it correct.
When selecting a room, it's important to read through all the details carefully before clicking the submit button. This is because once you submit your room selection, it may be difficult to make changes or cancel your current choice. Take the time to review the room type, bed size, roommates, and any additional information. If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to contact housing@nova.edu for clarification.

Additionally, it's important to double-check the dates of your room selection. Make sure the semester dates are accurate and that you are not accidentally selecting the wrong dates. This can save you the hassle of having to make changes or cancel your room selection altogether.

Before submitting your room selection, also take the time to review the cancellation policy. Understand the terms and conditions of cancelling your room selection, including any fees or penalties that may apply. Lastly, make sure to review the total cost of your room selection, including any taxes and fees. Check that the final price is within your budget and that you have selected the correct payment method. Once you have carefully reviewed all the details, you can confidently click the submit button.
